Dimension

How well does the film use subtext, symbolism, and metaphor to give the story a deeper meaning?

Breadth of Conflict

How well does the story’s driving conflict match the scope of the story in terms of its stakes?

Resolution Appropriateness

How well does the story’s resolution wrap up the conflict in a believable way?

Logic

Character Motivation

How much do the character motivations make sense for the characters as written and how well do they drive the characters’ actions?

Originality or Satisfaction of Adaptation
